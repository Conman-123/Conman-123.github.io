About Me

A little bit about me

My name is Connor Wiegmink and I’m currently studying Information Technology at the University of Queensland. I really enjoy programming and software development, and I have a strong history in problem solving and logical thinking subjects like physics and chemistry. I’m currently employed part time at a software development company, where I am constantly learning and gaining experience in this field. However, I also have a passionate creative side, with hobbies such as photography and digital 3D modelling. I love to bring these two skillsets together to create functional and beautiful pieces of software, tackling both creative complications and programming problems along the way.

Contact me at cwiegmink@outlook.com.

Projects

The Tale of Peter Rabbit

This project was part of UQ's DECO1400 Introduction to Web Design 2018 Semester One course. The task was to create an interactive story website for children aged 13 and under, utilising an existing story and transforming it into a digital experience. This project also served as my first real introduction into the world of web design.
